Pineapple and Lemon Juice Recipe

How to make a pineapple juice

This pineapple juice is one of my favorite juices. I like it because aside from the nutritional benefits, it takes less time to make

Today I will be sharing with you how I make this juice

Lists of ingredients for Pineapple juice

  • Fresh and sweet pineapple
  • A medium sized lemon
  • small sized ginger
  • Clean drinkable water

Steps to make my super simple pineapple juice

  1. Wash the pineapple, lemon and ginger thoroughly under running water
  2. Peel the skin and set aside in a clean bowl. Also peel the ginger skin if you want
  3. Cut open the pineapple and remove the core and add it to the pineapple peel
  4. Now cut the main pineapple into small chunks
  5. Place your pineapple and the ginger in a food processor, blend and set aside in clean bowl
  6. In a clean pot, pour in the pineapple peel and core, add some water and cook for 20 minutes
  7. Sieve your blended pineapple in a clothe sieve or thinly perforated sieve
  8. Extract the water from the cooked pineapple peel and pour the extracted water into the already sieved pineapple juice and stir properly
  9. Cut and squeeze the lemon juice into the pineapple juice and stir very well
  10. Leave to cook off in the refrigerator and serve chilled

Side note

The pineapple peel and core has great nutritional benefits that's why I don't just throw it away but you can do without it if you want
and your juice will still come out nicely.
