Grilled chicken recipe

how to make grilled chicken

I recently learnt how to grill chicken in an oven and ever since then I make grilled chicken every other weekend

Making grilled chicken is easy and I'm going to sharing my recipe with you.
You can make this any day of the week, during the weekends or when your friends come visiting and you don't know what to entertain them with

Ingredients for making grilled chicken

  • Whole soft chicken.
    Some breeds of chicken are easier to cook than others so you'd want to go for the one that is soft. You can use chicken parts if you want
  • Vegetable oil
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic and ginger powder
  • Onion powder
  • Thyme and curry
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt and vegetables for decoration (Any vegetable of choice)

Steps to prepare grilled chicken in an oven

  1. Wash your chicken thoroughly under running water and set aside
  2. Mix the garlic, ginger, onion powder. thyme and curry together in a dry bowl
  3. Add salt and seasoning cubes to the mix and mix thoroughly
  4. Put on a kitchen glove or use your hands if you want but because of the pepper in the mix, a glove will be your best bet
  5. Gently massage the whole chicken with vegetable oil. Remember the inner parts if you are using a whole chicken
  6. Now start applying the ingredient mix to every parts of the chicken. Make sure to coat every parts of the chicken with the mix
  7. Once you are done, place it in the fridge for 1-2hrs so it marinates a little
  8. Pre-heat your oven and place the chicken in the oven
  9. Take it off every 45 minutes and coat it with some vegetable oil. If needed, reapply the ingredient mix each time
  10. Once the chicken turns brown like the picture above, it should be fully cooked
  11. Take it out, place in a tray/big plate and decorate with the vegetables and serve

You can pair with some fried chips and ketchup
